About Crystal English Nursery and Primary School

Founded in September 2007 by a dedicated group of four women, Crystal English Nursery and Primary School (CENPS) has been committed to shaping the younger generation into future leaders.

With 18 years of academic excellence, our primary goal remains to nurture and equip students with knowledge, discipline, and values that will contribute to a better Cameroon. Over the years, CENPS has not only provided quality education but has also played a vital role in reducing unemployment by creating job opportunities for young professionals.

Our commitment to academic excellence is reflected in our 100% success rate in all public examinations, earning us recognition and respect in the educational sector.

At CENPS, we believe in creativity and talent development. We encourage students to explore and showcase their abilities in inter-school competitions, where they excel in academics, arts, and sports.

Our facilities include a state-of-the-art multimedia center and a spacious playground, ensuring that students have access to both learning and recreational opportunities.

Today, CENPS continues to thrive, with a team of 20 dedicated staff members and a security head, ensuring a safe and supportive environment for all learners.

Our Programs

Academics and Enrichment

Primary School students learn early literacy and math skills, as well as science and social studies concepts. Our enrichment program includes motor development, Spanish, drama, music, and library.

Assessment

We use assessments including observations, benchmarks, and portfolios to meet every child where they are and ensure they step forward at the pace that is right for them.

Parent Conferences

We hold parent-teacher conferences multiple times each year, and at other times at the teacher’s or parent’s request. Parents receive detailed reports on their child’s academic and personal development.

Parent Involvement

We encourage parents to take part in our students’ learning lives by volunteering as Homeroom Parents, for studio and class activities, and for St. Francis Parents Organization (SFPO) events.
