About Crystal English Nursery and Primary School

Founded in September 2007 by a dedicated group of four women, Crystal English Nursery and Primary School (CENPS) has been committed to shaping the younger generation into future leaders.

With 18 years of academic excellence, our primary goal remains to nurture and equip pupils with knowledge, discipline, and values that will contribute to a better Cameroon. Over the years, CENPS has not only provided quality education but has also played a vital role in reducing unemployment by creating job opportunities for young professionals.

Our commitment to academic excellence is reflected in our 100% success rate in all public examinations, earning us recognition and respect in the educational sector.

Creativity & Talent Development

At CENPS, we encourage pupils to explore and showcase their abilities in inter-school competitions, excelling in academics, arts, and sports.

Modern Facilities

Our school features a state-of-the-art multimedia center and a spacious playground, providing pupils with an enriching academic and recreational experience.

Safe & Supportive Environment

With a team of 19 dedicated staff members and a security head, we ensure that pupils thrive in a secure and nurturing learning atmosphere.

Our Programs

Academics and Enrichment

Primary School pupils learn early literacy and math skills, as well as science and social studies concepts. Our enrichment program includes motor development, Spanish, drama, music, and library.

Assessment

We use assessments including observations, benchmarks, and portfolios to meet every child where they are and ensure they step forward at the pace that is right for them.

Parent Conferences

We hold parent-teacher conferences multiple times each year, and at other times at the teacher’s or parent’s request. Parents receive detailed reports on their child’s academic and personal development.

Parent Involvement

We encourage parents to take part in our pupils’ learning lives by volunteering as Homeroom Parents, for studio and class activities, and for CENPS events.

Admissions

At Crystal English Nursery and Primary School, we welcome young learners to embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal growth. Our admissions process is designed to be seamless and inclusive, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to thrive in a nurturing and dynamic learning environment.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Medical report
  • Passport-sized photographs

How to Apply

Parents and guardians can obtain the admission form from our school office or download it from our website. Once completed, the form should be submitted along with the required documents. Our admissions team will review applications and schedule an assessment or interview where necessary.

Why Choose Crystal?

With a strong focus on academic excellence, creativity, and character building, we provide a holistic education that prepares pupils for future success. Join us today and be part of a school that nurtures potential and fosters lifelong learning.

Tuition

At Crystal English Nursery and Primary School, we strive to provide high-quality education at an affordable cost. Our tuition fees are structured to be accessible while maintaining our commitment to academic excellence and student development.

Payment Plans

We offer flexible payment plans, including:

  • Termly payments
  • Annual payments (discounts available)
  • Installment options for families in need

What’s Included?

Our tuition covers:

  • High-quality classroom instruction
  • Access to multimedia learning resources
  • Library and recreational activities
  • Participation in school-organized competitions

Financial Assistance

We provide scholarship opportunities and financial aid for qualifying pupils. Contact our administration office to learn more about eligibility and the application process.
